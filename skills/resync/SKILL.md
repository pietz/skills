I've drifted off and lost track of this session.
Give me a tight recap, no preamble, answering the questions:

1. Context — What are we working on and why?
2. Goal — What does "done" look like?
3. Progress — What's completed vs. still open?
4. Current step — What were you about to do next?
5. From me — Any decision or input you're waiting on?

Aim for one concise sentence per question. Not more than 2.
Use simple, conversational language.
